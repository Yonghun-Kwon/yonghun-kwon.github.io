Embedded Systems

Dept. of Computer Science and Engineering · Kangwon National University

Major Junior Fall

Embedded systems refer to computer systems that provide and receive information needed by humans in everyday living environments, as opposed to conventional desktop computers. Their applications span a wide range of domains, including mobile phones, smart home appliances, factory automation systems, intelligent buildings, and urban infrastructure. This course covers the fundamental structure and operation of embedded computer systems. In particular, students will develop skills as system software engineers by studying device driver programming for various components integrated into embedded systems, including LEDs, cameras, touch pads, and a variety of sensors.
